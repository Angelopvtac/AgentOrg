# Founder Profile

Not directly configured. Research agent reads the founder profile from `vault/founder-profile.json` to understand:
- Skills and expertise (what the founder can build/deliver)
- Goals and interests (what markets to research)
- Financial capacity and risk tolerance (what scale of opportunity to target)
- Time availability (what commitment level is realistic)
- Vision statement (the founder's stated direction)

All research is filtered through these founder constraints. A market opportunity that requires skills the founder doesn't have, capital they can't commit, or time they don't have is not a viable recommendation.
